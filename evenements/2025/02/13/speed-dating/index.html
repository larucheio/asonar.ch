After a short break, SONAR is back to shake up Geneva!

Geneva is full of connections you haven’t made yet. Spring is approaching, and if you’d like to share it with someone special, this Speed Dating event is for you.

If you’re not afraid to break the ice and meet new people, sign up quickly using the form – spots are limited. We’re planning for around 30 participants and will meet at the bar Le Grand Bain, Rue des Bains 33, 1205 Geneva.

Registrations are open until 1st of March 2025. Our goal is to arrange 5 to 7 matches per participant. We hope this event will be inclusive, but that will depend on the pool of registrants: there needs to be a sufficient number of potential matches (minimum 5 per person). Feel free to be precise and detailed when identifying the people you’re ready to meet! Once you’ve signed up, we’ll analyze the potential matches and confirm (or not) your participation. Registration is mandatory to participate!

Once your registration is confirmed by email from SONAR (by 5th March 2025 at the latest), we’ll send you the necessary information, including details for payment. The participation fee is CHF 15, and your first drink is on us (what a deal!).

Each "speed date" lasts 5 minutes. If we haven’t scheduled a match for you in a particular round, a table will be available to chat or mingle with the organizers. A short break will also be included midway through the event.
